County Comparisons: Out-of-Home Care

Help   Tutorial Video

This data visualization - a parallel coordinates plot - tells the story about out-of-home care in Washington State using a variety of factors. Read more or watch video.

 
 
 
Data: Population and poverty data is from the US Census Bureau. Child welfare data is extracted from FamLink by Children's Administration and provided to Partners for Our Children. This particular data was extracted on May 5, 2013. The data shown above includes all entries into out-of-home care between 2009 and 2011.
